Pizza Clicker!

How to Play

Click the pizza to earn pizzas (coins). Spend coins on upgrades to earn faster!

Upgrades

  • Oven – +1 pizza per click. Cost starts at 100.
  • Extra Cheese – +3 pizzas per click. Cost starts at 150.
  • Chef – +5 pizzas per click. Cost starts at 300.
  • Special Sauce – Starts an auto-clicker. Each purchase speeds it up. Cost starts at 200.

All upgrade costs increase by 1.5× after each purchase.

Rewards

  • First Slice – Make 50 pizzas total.
  • Pizza Apprentice – Make 100 pizzas total.
  • Bronze Baker – Make 400 pizzas total.
  • Silver Chef – Make 1000 pizzas total.
  • Pizza Master – Make 2000 pizzas total.
  • Upgrade Unlocked – Purchase your first upgrade.
